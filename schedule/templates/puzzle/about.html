Problem

CMU students must decide every semester which courses to enroll in. Many struggle to find a sequence of courses which will allow them to not only complete their degree requirements but also take the classes they want to with their favorite professors without overloading themselves academically.

Solution

This website allows CMU students to load their course requirements and upcoming-semester schedule options from Academic Audit and SIO. Students input their course preferences, favorite professors, and intended difficulty of semester schedule to the site. We’ll generate a set of schedules that both reflect these preferences and meet the student’s course requirements. By allowing students to evaluate their scheduling options according to both their long-term requirements and nearer-term preferences, this site will help them find the right balance between the courses they need and a schedule they enjoy.
